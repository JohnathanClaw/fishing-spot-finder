## Pro Tips: Times of Day, Water Type, and Gear

Maximize your chances with these expert tips:

### Best Times of Day

- **Bass:** Early morning and late evening, especially during warm months.
- **Trout:** Dawn and dusk, with midday action on overcast days.
- **Salmon:** Early morning or just before sunset during migration runs.

### Water Type

- **Bass:** Prefer warm, slow-moving lakes and ponds with plenty of cover.
- **Trout:** Thrive in cold, clear streams and rivers with rocky bottoms.
- **Salmon:** Migrate through large rivers and estuaries; look for deep pools and current breaks.

### Recommended Gear

- **Bass:** Medium-heavy rod, baitcasting reel, plastic worms, crankbaits.
- **Trout:** Light spinning or fly rod, small spinners, flies, or live bait.
- **Salmon:** Heavy-action rod, large spinning or trolling reel, spoons, plugs, or roe.

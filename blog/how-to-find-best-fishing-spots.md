## Tips for Evaluating Fishing Spots

Before you pack your gear, consider these factors to choose the best spot:

### Crowds

- **Check Popularity:** Some spots are busier than others. Use the app’s crowd indicators or visit during off-peak hours for a quieter experience.

### Accessibility

- **Assess Terrain:** Make sure the spot matches your mobility and equipment needs.
- **Parking and Facilities:** Look for amenities if you’re planning a longer outing or bringing family.

### Safety

- **Check Local Alerts:** Stay informed about weather, water conditions, and wildlife advisories.
- **Know the Area:** Review maps and user comments for any safety tips or hazards.
